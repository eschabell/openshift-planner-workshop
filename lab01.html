Installation

Business Resource Planner

  • Download and unzip.
  • Add product, see installs/README for details
    • $ cp jboss-bpms-brms-6.0.3.GA-redhat-1-optaplanner.zip business-resource-planner-workshop/installs/
  • Run init.sh or init.bat
    • $ ./init.sh
  • Read and follow instructions shown during installation.
